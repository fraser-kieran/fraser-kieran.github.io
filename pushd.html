The Pushd experiment aims to test alternate methods of creating and delivering push-notifications in order to improve the engagement experience for notification receivers. This is achieved by monitoring engagements with notifications and identifying patterns of preference for content and delivery.

This page facilitates pre-screening for the actual experiment. We need to ensure your device can receive notifications and you are able to engage with them. If you have modified any notification settings on your device, please reset them to default for the duration of this study. This experiment requires you to grant notification permissions. If you have not already done so, you will be prompted again once you tap the button labeled "Start Prescreen".

When you tap the "Start Prescreen" button, you should receive a notification titled "Prescreen Success". Tap the notification and you will be brought back to Prolific to confirm success and you will be able to start the experiment.

If you do not receive a notification, try tap the "Push Notification" button. If no notification arrives unfortunately you have failed the prescreen and are ineligible for the actual experiment. But thank you for trying! A link to prolific will be provided after 3 attempts. You may use this to complete the prescreen and claim your payment.
